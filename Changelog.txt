Reworked some content related to France as follows:
(1) The "Treaty of Paris" privilege is no longer removed through missions, but by annexing subjects. Each annexation of a subject in the French region reduces its strength by 20%; it is removed after annexing 5 subjects (for the two Prince paths). For the Military Government path and the Noble path, it will still be directly removed through the corresponding mission.
(2) On the Noble path (Uncle path), the missions "Night of Lesser Britain" and "Lorraine Crisis" can be completed diplomatically. Completing the mission "Call from Beyond" can also trigger the event "Redeem Calais" (if conditions are met).
(3) The autonomy reduction requirement in the mission "Call from Beyond" has been changed to increasing autonomy.
(4) The missions "Night of Lesser Britain" and "Lorraine Crisis" are no longer prerequisites for their subsequent missions.
(5) Fixed several bugs that could occur during the Southern Unification Assembly on the Noble path (Uncle path).
(6) The mission "Shatter the Channel League" can now also be completed by owning five provinces.
(7) The mission "Final Act of the Farce" no longer grants the reward to inherit the League of the Public Weal. Instead, it triggers an event to choose to turn them into incorporated vassals, maintain the status quo, or gain cores.
(8) When the privilege "Aristocratic Supremacy" is active, disasters for other estates (clergy and burghers) will not trigger.
(9) The mission "Revoke Privileges" has an added reward: you can call Appanages or Privileged Appanages into wars at half the favor cost (also applies to automatic call). The automatic call can now summon Privileged Appanages to war.
(10) Added a "skip life" option to skip the romantic side story.
(11) Strengthened AI France: it will now grant 10k troops to help deal with Feudal Vassal rebellions.
(12) The leader of the League of the Public Weal can no longer ally with themselves. The invitation to join the League of the Public Weal can no longer be used indefinitely.
(13) Added the option to choose which tag to play during the second civil war on the Military Government path (no buffs are inherited, and there is no additional content).
(14) The France Coalition disaster now requires 10 countries in the coalition for the progress to start. Peace: -0.8, War: +0.4, 10-20 members each contribute +0.1.
*The Noble path rework is not yet complete. Stay tuned for future updates.
